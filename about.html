Why Spicy Mayo?

To make spicy mayo accessible for everyone

Our Story

We started Free Spicy Mayo with the mission to make spicy mayo as attainable as soy sauce to all.

I don't know about you but we are avid sushi lovers. Lunch time? Sushi. Dinner date? Sushi. Dare I say it- breakfast? Usually not sushi but we can appreciate a breakfast sushi lover. We found there was always one daunting aspect about ordering sushi: Do we get spicy mayo and pay the extra FEE?! Here at Free Spicy Mayo we believe that you shouldn't even have to question whether or not you can afford the spicy mayo.

We have started this organization to partner with your favorite local sushi spot to help eat sushi dipped in spicy mayo till your hearts content
